Features

Workout Tracking

Log and track your exercises, sets, reps, and weights. Monitor your progress and see your strength improve over time.

Goal Setting

Set fitness goals for weight, strength, endurance, and habits. Track your progress and celebrate achievements.

Body Measurements

Track your weight, body fat percentage, and other body measurements to see your physical transformation.

Nutrition Tracking

Log your meals and track your calorie and macronutrient intake to support your fitness goals with proper nutrition.

Water Intake

Track your daily water consumption to ensure proper hydration, essential for optimal performance and recovery.

Analytics Dashboard

Visualize your progress with detailed charts and analytics to understand your fitness journey and make informed decisions.
